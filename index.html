Have a question? Check out the FAQ

We understand that starting a creative or digital project can come with a lot of questions. Below are some of the most common inquiries we get from our clients. If you don’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to reach out directly we’re happy to help.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with individuals, startups, small businesses, churches, and growing brands across a wide range of industries. Whether you’re just getting started or looking to rebrand, we tailor our services to fit your unique goals and vision.

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary depending on the service and complexity involved. A logo design might take 3–5 days, while a full website project can range from 2 to 4 weeks. We always set clear timelines upfront and keep you informed throughout the process.

Can I request changes after the project is delivered?

Yes! We value client satisfaction and include a set number of revisions in each project scope. If you need adjustments beyond that, we’re happy to discuss them and provide continued support where needed.

Do you offer ongoing support or maintenance?

Absolutely. We offer website maintenance packages, content updates, social media support, and more. Our goal is to ensure your digital presence stays fresh, secure, and effective even after launch.

How do I get started with Eaglesight Kreation?

Getting started is easy! Just click on the floating WhatsApp button to send us a WhatsApp message, or email us directly with a brief about your project. We’ll schedule a consultation and guide you through the next steps.

What makes Eaglesight Kreation different from other agencies?

We combine affordability with quality by working remotely and partnering with passionate creatives. Our focus is not just on delivery but on empowering both clients and team members. We bring heart, strategy, and creativity to every project big or small.
